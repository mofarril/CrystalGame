Crystals Collector!

    How to Play


  • You will be given a random number target at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your crystals total.
  • You win the game by matching the crystals total to the number target, you lose the game if the crystal total goes above the target number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
